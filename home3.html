Smart Infrastructure Services (SIS)

The Smart Infrastructure Services supports and guides UNDP Country Offices and partners to leverage technology
to deliver programmatic goals. By delivering a range of high-quality solutions and services for Green Energy
and ICT systems the team helps build local capacity and inspire innovation and encourage adoption across UNDP,
governments, NGO’s and the private sector.

Technology Innovation Services provide long-term information and communication technology solutions for UNDP
and UN partners aiming to integrate modern and reliable technology services TIS manages corporate applications
to enhance the user sign-in experience; Cloud hosting; global licensing services; Microsoft software subscriptions;
global network security operations for UNDP, and video conference and telephony solutions worldwide.
Smart Infrastructure Services is located in New York and Copenhagen.
